White Moose Head

The Edmonton- White Faux Moose Head Wall Hanging, the original sculpure by White Faux Taxidermy. Inspired by the natural wildlife surrounding our location in Utah, this faux moose head was created to appreciate nature. Each Edmonton moose is first cast from a limited-run template, then they are hand-painted using a matte white acrylic paint which renders more detail without overwhelming your space.
